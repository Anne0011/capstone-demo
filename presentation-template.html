Project Goals:

With my project I was aiming to provide a community curated database for:
  • Locals to post and share startup companies and their details
  • Collect and provide information on investors
  • Quickly identify places to work
  • Post jobs that need to be filled, and interesting news
  • Fill a need not currently well served in the Nashville startup community (distribution of information)

Lessons Learned:

  • To break things constantly is part of coding and iterating
  • Spacing and formatting matters if you want other people to help you (aka my son)
  • Learned more about using git collabortively
  • Always keep coding, even when you feel like it's too hard
  • It takes time to learn to code, it comes from scar tissue and repitition
  • Before you begin, you should definitely whiteboard and plan the structure of your app before starting
